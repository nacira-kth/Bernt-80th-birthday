About Bernt Øksendal

Professor Bernt Øksendal is a globally celebrated mathematician and pioneer in stochastic analysis and financial mathematics. His groundbreaking contributions, particularly in stochastic differential equations and their applications, have profoundly influenced research and education. His seminal textbook on stochastic differential equations remains a foundational resource for mathematicians worldwide.
